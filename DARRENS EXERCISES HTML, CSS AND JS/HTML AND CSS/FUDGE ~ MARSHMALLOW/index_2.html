Guinea Pigs do a lot of things like...

1) Sprawling 2)Wheeking 3)Chutting 4)Purring 5)Popcorning 6)Licking

(These are some good ones)

(what they mean)

1. Sprawling is when they lay down on there side/tummy and when they do that it tells you that they feel comfortable and that they feel safe around you.

2. Wheeking is when they're Excited to see you or when they can see that you're holding food.

3. Licking/nibbleing means they're licking your or nibbleing you to show there love and affection in other words grooming you. They're telling you that they care about your well-being. They also might lick you because you tase very tasty haha.

4. Popcorning means when they're very very happy/excited/having a really good time and jumping for joy:)

(And these are some not so good ones)

1. Chutting means that they could be angry,unhappy,or agitated at a certain situation.

2. Purring can either mean both things one is they could either be happy or angry you just have to read the signs there facial expressions.
